Abstract

There is a general construction producing a vector bundle on any smooth alge-
braic curve from a ’¼ünite-dimensional representation of the ┬½positive half┬╗ Vir+ of

projective line. We prove, in particular, that in this case the bundle is completely
determined by the action of the operators L0 and L1 and show how to compute it
explicitly (i.e., to ’¼ünd the decomposition into the direct sum of line bundles) knowing
the Jordan decomposition of L1. After that, we compute explicitly these bundles
for two families of representations of Vir+: the Fock modules and the Demazure
modules of level 1 corresponding to a multiple of the ’¼ürst fundamental weight for
the a’¼āne Kac-Moody algebra (cid:98)sl(n, C).
